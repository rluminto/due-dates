About

Deadline Scraper v1.0

Automatically tracks assignment deadlines from Gradescope and PrairieLearn.

Supported Sites:

  • Gradescope (*.gradescope.com)
  • PrairieLearn (*.prairielearn.com)

Usage Tips:

  • Visit assignment pages to automatically scrape deadlines
  • Use the refresh button in the popup to manually update from current page
  • Click on deadline items to open the assignment directly
  • Badge shows number of deadlines due in the next 24 hours
